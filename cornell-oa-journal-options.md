# Cornell OA Journal Options for the CHMM Paper

Source: https://guides.library.cornell.edu/openaccess/support (checked 2026-04-25)

The paper is a quantitative-finance / statistical-modeling work (CHMMs for equity returns, EM training, copulas). Below are the Cornell OA agreements that plausibly fit, ranked by fit.

## Strong fits (full APC waiver)

### Springer Nature hybrid journals
Most hybrid Springer titles, no APC for Cornell corresponding authors. Excludes fully-OA and Nature Portfolio titles.

Candidate journals that match scope:
- *Computational Economics*
- *Annals of Finance*
- *Decisions in Economics and Finance*
- *Computational Statistics*
- *Statistics and Computing*
- *Statistical Methods & Applications*
- *Digital Finance*

### Cambridge University Press
Full waiver on the OA option for all CUP journals.

Candidate journals:
- *Econometric Theory*
- *Macroeconomic Dynamics*
- *Probability in the Engineering and Informational Sciences*
- *Journal of Financial Econometrics* (verify publisher, may be Oxford rather than CUP)

### Royal Society
Full APC waiver through 2027. *Royal Society Open Science* is broad multidisciplinary and routinely takes computational/quantitative work, a clean fallback for a fast OA megajournal-style venue.

## Plausible but narrower

### ACM Open
No APC for ACM venues. Requires submission from an `@cornell.edu` email. Fits if the paper is reframed toward the algorithmic / ML angle.

Candidate journals:
- *ACM Transactions on Knowledge Discovery from Data*
- *ACM Transactions on Economics and Computation*
- *ACM Transactions on Modeling and Computer Simulation*

### Institute of Physics
Fits only with an econophysics framing, e.g. *Journal of Statistical Mechanics: Theory and Experiment* (page charges may still apply).

## Not relevant for this paper
ACS, RSC, Cold Spring Harbor, Karger, Society for Endocrinology, IWA, John Benjamins, Cogitatio, and the Subscribe-to-Open biomed / humanities programs (BioOne, APS, ASM, Berghahn, Project MUSE, Duke Demography, EDP A&A, White Horse, Annual Reviews).

## Recommendation

Top targets:
1. **Springer** *Computational Economics* or another quantitative-finance-adjacent Springer title
2. **CUP** *Econometric Theory* or *Macroeconomic Dynamics*
3. **Royal Society Open Science** as a fast multidisciplinary OA option

All three have zero APC for Cornell corresponding authors.
