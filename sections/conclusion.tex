A continuous HMM trained by Baum-Welch with quantile-based initialization reproduces the three Cont stylized facts simultaneously on SPY at two operating points: the held-out-likelihood and BIC choice $K^\star = 3$ for risk-management consumers (CHMM-N: $89.7\%$ IS / $80.1\%$ OoS KS, $|G_t|$ ACF-MAE $0.0467$) and a tail-fidelity panel at $K = 18$ for synthetic-data consumers ($93.6$--$95.6\%$ IS, $80.8$--$85.7\%$ OoS KS across four emission variants; Table~\ref{tab:model_comparison}). The mechanism is the closed-form mixture-of-eigenvalues identity for the absolute-return ACF (\citealp{hamilton1994time, krolzig1997markov, timmermann2000moments}); we apply it to recast the Ryd\'{e}n et al.\ low-$K$ failure as a rank statement on $\mathbf T - \mathbf 1 \bar{\boldsymbol\pi}^\top$ and show the rank binds only at $K \le 3$ Gaussian setups. The four emission variants share the same scaffold and land at four kurtosis points; CHMM-GED's per-state shape $p_k$ nests Gaussian and Laplace as boundary cases and produces a bulk/tail partition that replicates across seeds and tickers. VaR and ES envelopes bracket observed values at $1\%$ and $5\%$; a regime-conditional VaR that propagates the one-step-ahead state forecast through the CHMM mixture passes Kupiec, Christoffersen-ind, and Christoffersen-cc cleanly at every $(K, \alpha)$ on OoS (Table~\ref{tab:cond_var}), where every constant-across-$t$ generator rejects breach independence. Multi-asset synthesis composes through a rank-based Student-$t$ copula on the CHMM marginals at $\nu^\star = 6$ (six-asset IS off-diagonal correlation MAE $0.027$); on OoS the Gaussian and Student-$t$ copulas are statistically indistinguishable, with a quarterly rolling refit reducing the off-diagonal MAE to $0.185$ (Appendix~\ref{sec:rolling_copula_supp}).

Skew-heavy-tailed emissions and explicit-duration semi-Markov sojourns are companion-paper directions; scaling to larger $d$ via untruncated regular vines or factor copulas is the natural multi-asset follow-up.
